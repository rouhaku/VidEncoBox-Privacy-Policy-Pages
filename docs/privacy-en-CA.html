Privacy Policy

This app "VidEncoBox" may collect and use certain information to improve user experience and display advertisements.

1. Information We Collect

  • App usage data (launch count, error logs, etc.)
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Media files (video, audio, images) - stored only on your device
  • Project data (name, description, tags, etc.) - stored only on your device
  • Browsing history and download history - stored only on your device
  • Bookmarks and reading list - stored only on your device

2. Purpose of Use

  • Editing, converting, and encoding media files
  • Browsing web content and downloading media
  • Display of advertisements (using Google AdMob)
  • Crash and error analysis

3. External Data Transmission

This app uses the following external services:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google User Messaging Platform (GDPR/CCPA consent management)
  • Apple App Store (subscription payments)
  • SKAdNetwork (advertising attribution)

4. Tracking Permission

A tracking permission popup (App Tracking Transparency) may appear when the app launches for advertising purposes. Personalized ads will not be displayed unless the user grants permission.

5. Built-in Browser

This app includes a built-in browser for browsing web content and downloading media.

  • Browsing history, download history, and bookmarks are all stored only on your device
  • Cookies obtained from websites are used only for download processing and stored on your device
  • No browsing history is transmitted to external servers

6. Data Storage and Security

Data created and stored by this app is generally stored only on your device.

  • Media files and project data: stored in local storage on your device
  • Sensitive information: encrypted and stored using Keychain
  • iCloud sync: Project data is synced to iCloud only when enabled by the user
  • Folder lock feature: Protection using biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) is available

7. Device Permissions

This app may use the following device permissions:

  • Camera: Used for QR code scanning
  • Photo Library: Used for saving and loading screenshots and media

8. Subscriptions

This app offers in-app purchases (subscriptions). Payment processing is handled through Apple's App Store payment system, and we do not collect or store any payment information (such as credit card numbers). Premium members will have all advertisements removed.

9. Data Deletion

User-created data (media files, projects, history, etc.) can be deleted directly within the app. Uninstalling the app will delete all data stored on your device.
